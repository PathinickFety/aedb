Help & Support

Get assistance with AEDB Database. Find answers, tips, and contact information.

Getting Started

Dashboard Overview

The dashboard displays all programs and beneficiaries in your database. Use the action buttons to create new programs or add new beneficiaries to your system.

Creating a Program

Click "Create New Program" from the home page. Fill in program details including name, type, date, time, location, and responsible person. You can associate beneficiaries with the program during or after creation.

Adding Beneficiaries

Click "Add New Beneficiary" to register a new beneficiary. Enter personal information, contact details, address, family size, and upload a photo. You can assign beneficiaries to programs from the beneficiary detail page.

Managing Programs

View program details, edit information, assign beneficiaries, or delete programs. Click on any program from the list to access its management options.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I edit a program or beneficiary?

Click on the program or beneficiary name to view details, then click the "Edit" button. Make your changes and save. The last updated date will be automatically recorded.

How do I delete a program or beneficiary?

View the program or beneficiary details, then click the "Delete" button. You'll be asked to confirm the deletion. This action cannot be undone.

Can I upload a photo for beneficiaries?

Yes, you can upload a photo when creating or editing a beneficiary. If no photo is uploaded, a default icon will be displayed.

How do I associate beneficiaries with programs?

When creating or editing a program, select beneficiaries from the list. You can also manage associations from the program detail page by clicking "Edit Program".

Is there a dark mode available?

Yes! Click the theme toggle button in the top right corner of the navbar (🌙/☀️) to switch between light and dark modes. Your preference is saved automatically.

How are beneficiary records organized?

Beneficiaries are automatically sorted by creation date (newest first). Each record includes personal information, contact details, address, family size, category, and additional notes.

Can I track when records were created or modified?

Yes, each program and beneficiary record displays "Created" and "Last Updated" timestamps on the detail page.

What if I encounter an error?

Check your internet connection and ensure all required fields are filled correctly. If the issue persists, contact our support team for assistance.
